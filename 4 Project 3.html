Messi and Ronaldo

Dashboards

On dashboards you can see goals scored by Messi and Ronaldo. Interesting fact is that their score the most goals in the last minutes in matches. I changed goals scored in additional time to regular time (for example 47 to 45).

To create this project I used Power BI and Excel.
